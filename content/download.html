Most distributions ship with Xfce in their own packaging format, but if you want a newer version or you want to build Xfce from scratch, you can find the packages below. Also read: How to compile Xfce

Stable release 4.18

Xfce 4.18 is the most recent version of the Xfce desktop environment. Changes in this release .

  • https://archive.xfce.org/xfce/4.18/

Individual releases

Each package in Xfce can make individual stable or development releases, including the core packages (as described in the release model). You can follow the announcements of the releases on the Xfce users mailing list, watch the release feeds or follow the Xfce Mastodon or Twitter account.

  • https://archive.xfce.org/src/

Distributions

A lot of different distributions provide Xfce packages. Popular examples built around Xfce are Xubuntu, the Fedora Xfce Spin, Manjaro or Zenwalk, but there is a good chance your favorite distro provides packages as well.

You can find a more complete list at DistroWatch.com.
